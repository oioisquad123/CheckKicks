# Privacy Policy

**CheckKicks - AI Sneaker Authentication**

**Last Updated:** January 17, 2026

**Effective Date:** January 17, 2026

---

## Introduction

CheckKicks ("we," "our," or "us") operates the CheckKicks mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** (if using email sign-in)
- **Apple ID identifier** (if using Sign in with Apple)
- **Google account identifier** (if using Google Sign-In)
- **Display name** (optional, from your sign-in provider)

### 2. Photos and Images

To provide our sneaker authentication service, we collect:
- **Sneaker photographs** you upload for authentication (up to 6 images per check)
- These images are processed by our AI system and stored securely

### 3. Authentication Results

We store your authentication history, including:
- **Verdict** (Authentic, Suspicious, or Fake)
- **Confidence score** (percentage)
- **AI observations** about the sneaker
- **Date and time** of each authentication
- **Associated images** from the check

### 4. Purchase Information

When you purchase credits:
- **Transaction identifiers** from Apple's App Store
- **Credit balance** and purchase history
- **Product purchased** and amount

We do NOT collect or store your payment card details. All payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store.

### 5. Device Information

We may automatically collect:
- **Device type** and iOS version
- **App version**
- **Crash logs** and performance data (through Apple's analytics)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide the Service**
   - Process your sneaker photos through our AI authentication system
   - Display authentication results and history
   - Manage your credit balance

2. **Improve Our Service**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve AI accuracy
   - Fix bugs and optimize performance
   - Develop new features

3. **Communicate With You**
   - Send service-related notifications
   - Respond to support inquiries
   - Provide important updates about the App

4. **Process Transactions**
   - Fulfill credit purchases
   - Maintain transaction records

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers provided by Supabase, with data centers located in the United States. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- Encrypted data transmission (TLS 1.2+)
- Secure cloud storage with access controls
- Row-level security policies on our database
- Regular security updates and monitoring

### Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Authentication history:** Retained for 2 years or until you delete your account
- **Uploaded images:** Retained for 30 days after authentication, then automatically deleted
- **Purchase records:** Retained as required by law for financial records

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Export

You can view your authentication history within the App. To request a complete export of your data, contact us at support@checkkicks.app.

### Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by:
1. Going to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the App
2. Emailing support@checkkicks.app

Upon deletion request, we will:
- Delete your account within 30 days
- Remove your authentication history
- Delete associated images
- Retain only data required by law (financial records)

### Opt-Out

You can opt out of:
- Push notifications (via iOS Settings)
- Analytics (via iOS Settings > Privacy > Analytics)
